Platform Event Thinking

Real-Life Example

Scenario

When a student completes fee payment in the College Management System.

Systems/Users Automatically Notified

* Accounts department receives payment confirmation
* Student portal updates fee status
* Email service sends payment receipt
* SMS service sends confirmation message
* Administration dashboard updates payment records

Why This is Important

This ensures all connected systems stay updated instantly without manual communication.

It improves accuracy, reduces delays, and supports real-time enterprise operations.
