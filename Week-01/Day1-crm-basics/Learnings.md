Day 1 Learnings

---

1. Salesforce Basics

* Learned that Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform.
* Understood how Salesforce helps businesses manage customers and sales processes.
* Learned that Salesforce is widely used by companies for automation and customer relationship management.

---

2. CRM Concepts

* Learned that CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.
* Understood the importance of maintaining customer relationships in businesses.
* Learned how CRM helps companies organize customer information and communication.

---

3. Business Workflow Understanding

Learned the Salesforce customer lifecycle:

Lead → Contact → Opportunity → Customer

Understanding

* Lead = Potential customer
* Contact = Confirmed person
* Opportunity = Possible business deal
* Customer = Successful customer

---

4. Salesforce Objects and Records

* Learned that Objects are database tables in Salesforce.
* Learned that Records are individual entries inside objects.
* Understood standard Salesforce objects such as:

  * Account
  * Contact
  * Lead
  * Opportunity

---

5. Salesforce Roles

Salesforce Admin

* Manages Salesforce platform configuration.
* Creates reports, dashboards, and workflows.

Salesforce Developer

* Builds custom applications and features.

* Writes Apex and Lightning code.

* Learned the difference between Admin and Developer roles.

---

6. Trailhead and Playground

* Learned how to use Trailhead for Salesforce learning.
* Understood the importance of Salesforce Playground.
* Learned that Playground is a safe environment for practice and experimentation.

---

7. Real-World Understanding

Understood how Salesforce can be used in a College Management System.

Real-world Mapping

* Lead → Student inquiry
* Contact → Student
* Opportunity → Admission process
* Customer → Joined student

---

8. Overall Learning Outcome

By the end of Day 1:

* Understood Salesforce fundamentals.
* Learned CRM basics and business workflow.
* Understood Salesforce objects and records.
* Learned difference between Salesforce Admin and Developer.
* Gained basic hands-on experience with Trailhead Playground.
