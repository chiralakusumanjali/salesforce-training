❓ Day 1 – Questions, Clarifications, and Understanding

Salesforce Platform Questions

1. Why do companies prefer Salesforce over traditional software?

Salesforce works completely on cloud technology, which allows companies to access data from anywhere without installing heavy software on local systems.

2. How does Salesforce organize huge amounts of customer information?

Salesforce stores information in structured objects and records, making it easier to manage millions of customer details efficiently.

3. How is customer information protected in Salesforce?

Salesforce uses security features like:

* Login authentication
* User permissions
* Access control
* Data encryption

to secure business information.

---

🔹 CRM and Workflow Questions

4. Why is CRM important for businesses?

CRM helps organizations improve communication with customers and manage sales processes in a systematic way.

---

5. What happens after a Lead becomes successful?

When a lead is verified and business interaction begins, it can be converted into a Contact and Opportunity.

---

6. Can a single company have multiple contacts?

Yes. One organization can contain many contacts.

Example:

A college may have:

* Students
* Faculty
* Staff members

all connected to the same Account.

---

7. Why are Opportunities important in Salesforce?

Opportunities help businesses track deals, monitor progress, and estimate future sales.

---

🔹 Salesforce Development Questions

8. What skills are needed for Salesforce development?

A Salesforce Developer should learn:

* Apex programming
* Lightning framework
* JavaScript basics
* Salesforce customization
* Database concepts

---

9. What is the purpose of Apex?

Apex is used to create custom business logic and automation inside Salesforce applications.

---

10. Why are Lightning Components used?

Lightning Components help developers build interactive and reusable user interfaces in Salesforce applications.

---

11. How are Salesforce projects tested before release?

Developers usually test applications in sandbox or testing environments before deploying them to real business systems.

---
