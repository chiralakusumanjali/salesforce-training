Revision Questions and Detailed Answers

1. Why is clean data important?

Clean data is accurate, complete, consistent, and reliable information stored within a system. Enterprise organizations depend on clean data for reporting, decision-making, automation, and daily operations.

Benefits of clean data:

* Accurate reports
* Better business decisions
* Improved efficiency
* Better customer service
* Reduced operational risk

Without clean data, organizations may make incorrect decisions and experience operational problems.

---

2. What problems happen because of duplicate records?

Duplicate records occur when the same information exists multiple times within the system.

Examples:

* Same student entered twice
* Same customer registered multiple times

Problems caused by duplicates:

* Duplicate emails and notifications
* Inaccurate reporting
* Confusion among employees
* Poor customer experience
* Increased storage costs

Duplicate records reduce trust in system data and create operational inefficiencies.

---

3. Why is data migration difficult?

Data migration involves transferring information from one system to another.

Example:

Migrating student records from Excel to Salesforce.

Challenges include:

* Duplicate records
* Missing information
* Invalid data
* Inconsistent formats
* Relationship mapping issues

Migration requires careful planning, validation, testing, and verification to ensure successful data transfer.

---

4. What is Data Loader used for?

Data Loader is a Salesforce tool used to manage large volumes of data.

Functions include:

* Insert records
* Update records
* Upsert records
* Delete records
* Export records

Data Loader is commonly used for:

* Data migration
* Bulk updates
* Backup operations
* Enterprise data management

It helps organizations process thousands of records efficiently.

---

5. Why should enterprises validate imported data?

Data validation ensures that imported information meets business standards before entering the system.

Validation helps:

* Prevent invalid records
* Improve data quality
* Reduce errors
* Protect system integrity

Examples:

* Attendance cannot exceed 100%
* Email must follow proper format
* Required fields cannot be empty

Validation prevents bad data from affecting enterprise operations.

---

6. Why are CSV formats important?

CSV (Comma Separated Values) files are widely used for importing and exporting data between systems.

Advantages:

* Simple structure
* Easy to create
* Compatible with many systems
* Efficient for bulk data processing

Salesforce Data Loader commonly uses CSV files because they provide a standardized format for transferring data.

---

7. What risks happen during bulk import?

Bulk imports involve processing large numbers of records simultaneously.

Risks include:

* Duplicate records
* Incorrect field mapping
* Missing data
* Invalid values
* Data corruption
* Reporting errors

Because thousands of records can be affected at once, organizations must test imports carefully before performing large-scale operations.

---

8. Why is governance important in data management?

Data governance refers to the policies, controls, standards, and procedures used to manage enterprise data.

Governance ensures:

* Data quality
* Security
* Compliance
* Accountability
* Reliability

Benefits include:

* Better decision-making
* Reduced business risk
* Improved transparency
* Regulatory compliance

Without governance, enterprise data becomes inconsistent, unreliable, and difficult to manage.
