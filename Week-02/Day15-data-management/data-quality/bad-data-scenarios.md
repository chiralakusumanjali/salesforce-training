10 Bad Data Scenarios and Business Impact

## 1. Duplicate Student Records

Problem:
Same student exists multiple times.

Business Impact:

* Duplicate notifications
* Incorrect reports
* Attendance confusion

---

## 2. Missing Email Address

Problem:
Student email not available.

Business Impact:

* Important notifications fail
* Communication breakdown

---

## 3. Wrong Department Assignment

Problem:
Student assigned to incorrect department.

Business Impact:

* Incorrect course allocation
* Reporting inaccuracies

---

## 4. Invalid Attendance Percentage

Problem:
Attendance recorded as 120%.

Business Impact:

* False reports
* Incorrect academic decisions

---

## 5. Duplicate Course Allocation

Problem:
Student enrolled twice in same course.

Business Impact:

* Data inconsistency
* Scheduling conflicts

---

## 6. Incorrect Phone Number

Problem:
Contact information invalid.

Business Impact:

* Emergency communication failure

---

## 7. Missing Fee Information

Problem:
Payment records incomplete.

Business Impact:

* Revenue tracking issues
* Financial reporting errors

---

## 8. Incorrect Scholarship Status

Problem:
Scholarship awarded to wrong student.

Business Impact:

* Financial loss
* Student complaints

---

## 9. Invalid Date Format

Problem:
Different date formats used.

Business Impact:

---

## 10. Missing Faculty Information

Problem:
Faculty records incomplete.

Business Impact:

* Scheduling problems
* Operational inefficiencies

---

## Conclusion

Bad data reduces system reliability and creates serious business risks.

Poor-quality data can lead to:

* Incorrect business decisions
* Operational inefficiencies
* Financial problems
* Communication failures
* Reduced customer trust
* Compliance risks

Enterprise organizations must maintain clean, accurate, and reliable data through:

* Validation rules
* Duplicate prevention
* Data governance
* Regular audits
* Proper user training

High-quality data is essential for successful enterprise operations and long-term organizational reliability.
